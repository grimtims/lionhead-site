Exteriors

There is something special about what one feels about the exterior of their home. It represents who they are, their values and often is a window to their backgrounds. Our objective is to help select the right materials, colours and to develop and build the details which give the home that distinctive look that they have been dreaming of. That's craftsmanship and we're so proud of our ability to be a part of helping you materialize your dreams.

Living Spaces

You spend a lot of time in your home. Some rooms are just a more important than others for living, playing or entertaining. Craftsmanship and attention to detailing in these areas are so important in projecting your taste and what you care about. We bring to you a skilled team of people to create these spaces.

Kitchens

Luxury today is being at home and enjoying well prepared, well presented wholesome meals. Whether you're grabbing something hearty as you're off to the rink, whipping up dreamy poached eggs on Sunday morning or creating mouthwatering masterpieces for friends, kitchens have become centres for living. We have worked with our clients and with kitchen designers to bring to "the table" the spaces, materials, lighting and appliances that meet your lifestyle needs.

Bathrooms

Remember the bathroom in your first apartment? Gone are the days of those minimal and basic rooms. Today, space, luxury and comfort are in vogue. Rich woods, decorative tiles and stones, and good lighting are important components in these new spa like environments. Lionhead can discuss with you and provide you with samples of the materials for your consideration. We can advise on durability, quality and maintenance to help make your decisions easier.

Sketches

Architects and designers can create impressive 3D renderings of your design. Most lack character; the really good photographic ones come at a price. But, we like those old fashion handed drawn images of your new home. They're fast and because of this, we can very quickly generate several options depicting different colours and materials. These create interesting conversations. We think that these drawings have more character and atmosphere than the computer drawings and in the end, some clients have been known to frame these quaint studies. We like to think they have the charm of those famous French Impressionists painters.
